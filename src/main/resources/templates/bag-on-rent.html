Rules and Guidelines

1.

Customer taking Trolley Bag on rent is to use this for personal use only (not for commercial)

2.

Customer to read carefully all the Rules and Regulations.

3.

Customer to give hard copy of their Aadhar Card with Present Address and 2 mobile nos.

4.

Customer to pay Deposit & Rent in advance

5.

Customer to check the condition of bag/s i.e. wheels, lock, handle, zeep, body etc before signing of this form.

6.

The day issued and the day returned the bag, both will be considered a full day.

7.

Customer to return the bag/s on date confirmed and mentioned on the form.

8.

Any delay, the rent will be calculated @ Rs.100/- per day and deducted from deposit amount.

9.

While returning the bag/s, any damage to bag, the repairing charged based on damage will be recovered from customer & will be deducted from Deposit amount.

10.

In case of lost of bag, fully damaged, customer has to compensate the cost of the bag.

11.

If bag/s are not available, customer has to register their name with details with token amount & accordingly we will call them on availability of bag/s. This will be available on first registered first served basis.
